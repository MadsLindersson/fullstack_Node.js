## Planets

| HTTP Verb | URL          | Operation                        |
|-----------|--------------|----------------------------------|
| GET       | /planets     | Retrieves all planets.           |
| GET       | /planets/{id}| Retrieves 1 planet based on id.  |
| POST      | /planets     | Creates 1 planet.                |
| PUT       | /planets/{id}| Updates 1 planet.                |
| DELETE    | /planets/{id}| Deletes 1 planet.                |
